## Output-Type Presets

Pick one primary type before retrieval:

1. `review`
- Typical sections: background, taxonomy, comparative analysis, gaps, future work.

2. `course_report`
- Typical sections: problem statement, methods overview, selected evidence, discussion, conclusion.

3. `experiment_report`
- Typical sections: objective, setup, procedure, results, error analysis, conclusion.

4. `design_proposal`
- Typical sections: requirements, architecture/options, tradeoffs, plan, risk.

5. `thesis_chapter`
- Typical sections: chapter objective, related work thread, method/details, chapter summary.

## Keyword Quality Checklist

- Keep each group 5-12 terms.
- Include abbreviations and full names.
- Include at least one mechanism/method group.
- Add bilingual synonyms only when multilingual search is enabled.
- Remove terms that are too generic to rank relevant papers.
